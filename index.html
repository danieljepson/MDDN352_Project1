Panorama Timelapse Photo Gallery

What is it

This is app collates photos that people take of the weather around them and lines them up to make it look like a panorama picture but made from many different images so in a way it will look like a collage of the city that the photos are taken from.

Who is it for

The app will be avalible in any mobile and table device so it can be used by anyone anywhere so that there is a wide range of photos to collate and give a better picture of the weather in that city. As for the people who will use the app, it is designed so that anyone can use it because it is simple to do.

How it is used

When someone takes a photo using the app it is uploaded to the database with the location data and then is lined up depending on the location to make a panorama image.
